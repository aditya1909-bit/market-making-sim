Card market

How this game runs

Information

Each player starts with 2 private cards from a normal 52-card deck, and that private hand stays fixed for the full round.

Trading

Anyone can keep a live quote in the room, and anyone else can buy or sell against it while it is live.

Clock

Rounds run on a timer. New board cards reveal automatically, or earlier if the whole room votes to speed it up.
